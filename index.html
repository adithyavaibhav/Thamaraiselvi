About Dr. Thamarai Selvi

Dr. R. Thamarai Selvi is a seasoned professional with more than a decade of experience in Human Resource Training. She has been a Knowledge Facilitator in renowned business schools and corporate houses in Coimbatore for a long period. An enterprising individual with versatile knowledge and rich expertise in both Academic and Corporate sectors.

She has successfully conducted more than 200 "tailor-made" sessions that include workshops, webinars, interactive lectures, and training calendars. These sessions have been highly interesting, informative, and inspirational to all kinds of audiences. The learning sessions are always interactive with simple activities, managerial exercises, team-building games, effective case studies, and useful videos.

Dr. Thamarai Selvi specializes in transforming individuals and organizations through her unique training methodologies that focus on Attitude, Behavior, and Competency development.
